Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Fettuccine Alfredo is a classic Italian dish that you can make at home with just a few simple ingredients. The dish was created in the early 19th century by Alfredo Di Lelio of Italy, where it is known as fettuccine al burro.

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • ¾ pint heavy cream
  • table salt and black pepper
  • garlic salt
  • ¾ cup grated Romano cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of lightly salted water.
  2. Add pasta to water, then cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente.
  3. In a large saucepan, melt butter into heavy cream over low heat. Add salt, garlic salt, and pepper.
  4. Increase saucepan heat to medium, then stir in Romano and Parmesan cheese until melted.
  5. Once sauce has thickened, add cooked pasta. Toss until coated, then serve.
